Taste the feeling

Kabucha is more than a fine dining restaurant.we offer a variety of dishes from all over the world. Our process isn’t mechanical but thought and emotion driven.Our menu is curated with you in mind,our food feels like a cozy rainy night, a tropical beach , a gentle hug and more. It’s not about eating your feelings but food that makes you feel.

starter

Begin your experience with thoughtfully prepared starters designed to awaken the appetite without overwhelming it. From creamy mushroom soup finished with truffle oil, to crispy calamari, fresh garden salads, and lightly spiced chicken wings, each dish balances flavor, texture, and comfort—setting the perfect tone for what’s to come.

main course

Our main courses are crafted to feel both satisfying and memorable. Enjoy slow-braised beef short ribs, herb-grilled chicken, rich seafood pasta, or perfectly seasoned vegetarian risottos. Every plate is generous, deeply flavorful, and designed to leave you feeling nourished, content, and truly indulged.

Dessert

End on a sweet note with desserts that feel like a reward. From warm chocolate fondant with a molten center, to classic crème brûlée, cheesecake, and seasonal fruit tarts, our desserts are rich yet balanced—comforting without being heavy, and always worth savoring.

Alcohol & beavarages

From our curated alcohol selection to those who prefer non-alcoholic options we offer, fine wines, classic cocktails, premium whiskies, and signature house drinks,brewed coffee, specialty teas, fresh juices, handcrafted mocktails, and refreshing soft drinks to complements every dish on the menu.
